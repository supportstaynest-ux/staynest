Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset a vendor's password? expand_more
To reset a vendor's password, go to the 'Vendors' tab in your dashboard, select the specific vendor profile, and click on 'Account Settings'. From there, you can trigger a password reset email or set a temporary one.
Can I export monthly financial reports? expand_more
Yes, navigating to the 'Reports' section allows you to filter by date range. You can download these reports in PDF, CSV, or XLSX formats directly from the 'Export' button in the top right.
What are the platform fees for high-volume vendors? expand_more
Platform fees are tiered based on transaction volume. Vendors exceeding $50k/month are eligible for our 'Enterprise Plus' tier which reduces fees by 0.5%. Contact your account manager for more details.
